Statistics Documentation

The statistics page is dedicated to provideing the user information visually regarding the stats of each establishments based on either a map showing which areas passed a test, the records of each establishment, or the amount of records for each type of inspections done.

The Statistics page is interactive, and initially shows the user a bar graph of the establishments vs the amount of records that said establishment has had based on the data found in our API. In order to view more statistics that we created to show off our data, click on the arrows to toggle through the different visualizations.

We believe that the bald spot in these graph is due to the lack of testing going on in that area.

There are graphs where the Y-axis is the "Number of Records" (which represents how many instances that each x-axis category showed up) vs either the establishments, the type of establishment or the years recorded. And the Georgraphical map shows the regions of Prince Georges county that was tested and the specific areas that failed a specific test.

Establishments

The Establishment tab hold the information of the specific food establishment that you (user) wants to know more about. It will have the name of the establishment, the location of it, the current and previous inspection reports and the type of establishment it is

You could use this information to see try and find some new places to eat if you are feeling adventurous, but not reckless to go to any place. With this tab, you can find the closest restaurants near the location you want and see if the food is "fresh" enough to ensure that you won't get an upset stomach after eating there.
