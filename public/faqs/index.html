FAQs

Frequently Asked Question:

Q: How do I use the site?
A: You can view the map of the College Park area based on the specifications that the user desires to view through the filter option.

Q: How accurate is the data?
A: We pulled the data from the PG county database that is constantly being updated, so we know from that the data we have on the website is up to date.

Q: What can I do with the site?
A: The map on the front page is interactive, so you can load information on the previous or current status of a restaurants health inspection, as well as the stores address and its location on the map.
