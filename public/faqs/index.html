FAQs

Frequently Asked Question:

Q: How do I use the site?
A: You can view the map of the College Park area based on the specifications that the user desires to view through the filter option.

Q: How accurate is the data?
A: We pulled the data from the PG county database that is constantly being updated, so we know from that the data we have on the website is up to date.

Q: What can I do with the site?
A: The map on the front page is interactive, so you can load information on the previous or current status of a restaurants health inspection, as well as the stores address and its location on the map.

Statistics Documentation

Q: What is the "Statistics" tab, and how do I use it?
A: The statistics page is dedicated to provideing the user information regarding which location within the district which district has passed or not for the specific tag.

Q: How to Use the "Statistics" tab
A: On the shaded map of Prince Georges county, there are various different types of inspections that are run in each establishments. There are a total of nine different inspections, with differing results. When a county is lit up, that means that they matched the type of test ran, for example if it is lit for Facility Closed, then the last test of that establishment ended in them closing for a bit or permantly. If not lit then they did not match that test.

Q: What is that blank spot on every graph in "Statistics"?
A: We believe that the bald spot in every graph is due to the lack of testing going on in that area.
